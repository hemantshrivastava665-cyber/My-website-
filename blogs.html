Mobile se paisa kamana sach hai ya scam?

Aaj kal tumne har jagah ek cheez zaroor dekhi hogi — “Mobile se paise kamao ” “Ghar baithe earning karo ” “Sirf phone se ₹5000 daily ” Sach bolu? Pehli baar jab maine bhi ye sab dekha tha, mujhe laga — “Bhai life set ho gayi 😎” Lekin… reality thodi different nikli

Problem kya hai?

Internet par 2 types ke log hote hain: Ek jo genuinely sikhate hain Aur dusre jo bas sapne bechte hain Aur dikkat ye hai ki… 90% cheeze jo tum dekhte ho — wo ya to incomplete hoti hain ya misleading Example: “1 din me ₹1000 kamao” “App download karo aur paisa aayega” Sach me aisa hota to sab rich hote na?

To kya sab scam hai?

Nahi. Sab scam nahi hai. Lekin sab easy bhi nahi hai. Real earning hoti hai… Skill se Time se Consistency se
